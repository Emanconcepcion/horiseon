# Horriseon

## Description 

Horiseon is a fully operating website that explains how utalizing Search Engine Optimization, Online Reputation Management, Social Media Marketing, Lead Generation, Brand Awareness and Cost Management will boost the success of your business.

* Search Engine Optimization= explains how optimizing the use of search engine will increase your visibility and find the right customers for you business
* Online Reputation Management: the control over what potential customer will see when they search for your business.
* Social Media Maketing= helps determine which platforms are best suited for your brand. With the increase of social media it will increasingly influence the buying habits of people by using analytics to find the right market for you.
* Lead Generation= the strategies the will lessen the work requirement for your business in tern will increase the customers that directly goes to your website.
* Brand Awareness= Increasing the search ranking and visibility for you business will allow users to find your business through paid and organic searches.
* Cost Management= As your search ranking for your business increases the cost for advertisement decreases, since you will no longer need advertisement for your page.
